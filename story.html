The Aroama Story

When you use aroama.

Through aromatherapy, Aroama provides automated, immediate relief when you experience stress or anxiety. Each day, you will recievve a comprehensive report of your stress levels throughout the day. With the option to connect to your calendar and GPS, the reports will include when and where you were when you experienced the most stress that day. Long term reports will also be generated to show trends over time.

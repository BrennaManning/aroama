Product Features

What is araoama and what makes it unique?

Aroama is an aromatherapy wearable that tracks your stress levels. When you experience stress, Aroama will emit a soothing scent into the air around it. Additionally, Aroama's application will keep track of when and where you tend to experience stress or anxiety. Aroama provides instant relief as well as the information necessary to reflect and work towards a better lifestyle for long term stress management.


Sleek Design

Made out of elastomer, the Aroama Band has a nice velvety hand-feel and aesthetically looks like fitness wearables.

Scent Diffusion Options

Aroama users can choose which mode they prefer for their wristband. The aromatherapy scent diffusion can be done either automatically when stress is detected, or manually released whenever the user chooses. There is of course also a mute option for whenever the user would rather not have any scent released.



Automatic Diffusion: Once the Aroama Band detects when a user is anxious, the inserted scent is automatically dispensed into the air, allowing a hands-free, on-the-go aromatherapy experience.
Manual Diffusion: Users can use the accompanying app to manually control the desired strength of scents and control the timing of the scent being released with the press of a button.

Scent Variety

Users can choose from a wide range of removable scent pods that cater to their preferences. These pods will be replaced every couple weeks, so you can experiment with differnt scents to see which work best for you.
Aroama's most popular aromas are lavender, chamomille, mint, and eucalyptus.
